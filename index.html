About Me

My name is Hoang Quoc Cuong, and I am a software engineer with a strong enthusiasm for technology and creative problem-solving. I value clear communication and collaboration, and I enjoy working in environments where ideas can be shared freely and developed together. Being able to cooperate smoothly with others while continuously learning from different perspectives is an important part of how I grow as a developer.

Most of my programming experience comes from academic projects completed throughout my coursework. I have worked on projects such as Android applications, software systems, and full-stack web applications.

I have experience working with multiple programming languages including Java, C++, C#, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Through these projects I continue strengthening my technical foundation and expanding my understanding of software engineering.

My long-term goal is to build a creative and engaging game that combines technology, imagination, and thoughtful design. Outside of programming, I also enjoy music, especially listening to and writing rap lyrics, which gives me another creative outlet.
